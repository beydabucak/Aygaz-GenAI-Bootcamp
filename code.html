About the Author, Beyda Bucak

As I pen down the chapters of my life, I find myself at the intersection of two seemingly divergent worlds: computer engineering and literature. Born with a curious mind and an insatiable thirst for knowledge, I embarked on a journey of exploration that would shape the course of my life. Growing up in a small town, I was drawn to the wonders of technology from a young age.
The allure of computers and programming languages fascinated me, igniting a passion that would lead me to pursue a degree in computer engineering.
As I delved deeper into the intricacies of algorithms and software development, I discovered a profound appreciation for the elegance of code and the boundless possibilities it offered.
Yet, amidst the sea of binary digits and lines of code, there existed another realm – a realm of imagination and creativity. Fuelled by my love for storytelling and literature, I embarked on a parallel journey as an aspiring author. With each stroke of the pen, I found solace in the worlds I created, weaving tales that transported readers to distant lands and uncharted territories.
Join me as I embark on this extraordinary journey – a journey of discovery, innovation, and creativity. Together, let us explore the boundless horizons of the human mind and chart a course towards a future filled with endless possibilities.
